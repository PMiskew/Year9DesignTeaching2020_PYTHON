What Is Quality Education?



Odds are that if you are reading this, you probably have some form of formal education. However many kids cannot attend school, because of where they live, or because of a lack of enforcement. Eductation is a right to everyone, and no matter the circumstances everyone should be able to attend some form of school, online or in person.
